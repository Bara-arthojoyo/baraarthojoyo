Humus Solid (Organic Fertilizer)

Humus Solid is an organic fertilizer derived from the natural decomposition of plant-based and animal-based materials. It is rich in stable organic matter, primarily humic acids, fulvic acids, and beneficial microorganisms that improve the physical, chemical, and biological properties of the soil.

This product enhances soil fertility by increasing cation exchange capacity (CEC), stimulating root development, and improving soil aeration and water retention. Its high carbon content also plays a critical role in long-term soil structure improvement and nutrient holding capacity.
