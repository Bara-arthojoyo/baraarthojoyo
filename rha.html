RICE HUSK ASH

Learn more

Rice Husk Ash (RHA) is a highly valuable byproduct obtained from the controlled combustion of rice husks. Rich in silica (SiO₂), this fine, grayish powder is widely used in various industries including construction, agriculture, and manufacturing. Its pozzolanic properties make it an excellent additive for cement and concrete, improving strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals.

In agriculture, RHA serves as a sustainable soil amendment, enhancing soil aeration, pH balance, and nutrient retention. It is also applied as an eco-friendly filler, insulation material, and even in water filtration processes. With its abundant supply and renewable nature, Rice Husk Ash represents a cost-effective and environmentally conscious solution for multiple industrial applications.
